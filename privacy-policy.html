Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 15 April 2026

Apna Hisab ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimal information required to provide core functionality:

  • Personal Information: Name and email address (collected via Google Sign-In)
  • App Data: Financial transactions (income and expenses) entered by the user

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Provide personalized features such as profile, referral, and premium access
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the app

3. Data Storage

  • All financial transaction data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Basic user information (name and email) is securely stored using Firebase services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (for secure login)
  • Firebase Firestore (for storing basic user information)

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data using industry-standard practices provided by Firebase.

7. Children's Privacy

Apna Hisab is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

8. Future Features

Some features such as SMS-based expense tracking are currently under development and may be introduced in future updates. Appropriate permissions and disclosures will be provided before enabling such features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on this page.
