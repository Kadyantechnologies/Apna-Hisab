🚀 Apna Hisab
Your Money. Your Device. Your Control.

A privacy-first, local-first personal finance app built for India 🇮🇳
Track your income and expenses automatically—without giving up your data.

✨ Why Apna Hisab?

Most finance apps ask for access to your financial data…
and then upload it to their servers.

Apna Hisab is built differently.

🔐 No cloud storage

📴 Works completely offline

🚫 No login required

❌ No ads. No data selling

👉 Your financial data stays on your device—always.

⚡ Features
📩 Automatic Transaction Tracking

Detects and extracts transactions directly from bank SMS.

📊 Smart Dashboard

Get a clear overview of:

Total Balance

Income

Expenses

🧠 Budget Advisor

Uses the 50-30-20 rule to help manage spending and savings.

📈 Category-wise Analytics

Understand where your money goes with visual insights.

📝 Manual Entry

Add cash transactions or missed entries easily.

🔐 Privacy First Architecture

All processing happens on-device—no external servers involved.

🧩 How It Works

Install the app

Grant SMS permission (processed locally)

Transactions are automatically detected

View insights instantly

No setup. No login. No complexity.

🏗️ Tech Stack

Framework: Flutter (Dart)

Database: SQLite / Hive (local storage)

Design: Material 3 + Glassmorphism UI

Processing: On-device Regex & heuristic engines
